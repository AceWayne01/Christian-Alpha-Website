Our Story

Christian Alpha was born from the belief that style is more than what you wear — it’s a statement of who you are and what you stand for.

We mix casual, street, and custom wear to create pieces that speak confidence, faith, and originality. Every design carries the message that your future isn’t written for you — you create it, one bold step at a time.

“Wear it with pride.” — Founder

Mission

To inspire confidence and purpose through fashion that blends faith, strength, and modern street culture.

Vision

To become a leading African streetwear brand known for quality custom designs representing faith and originality.

Values
